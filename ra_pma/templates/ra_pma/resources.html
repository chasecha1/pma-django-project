Helpful Items

  • TransLoc: A real-time bus tracking system that also provides access to SafeRide, a late-night transportation service for students.
  • Grubhub: A food delivery and pick-up platform that integrates with campus dining for meal exchanges and flexible payment options.
  • Speed Queen: A laundry management app that allows you to check machine availability, start laundry cycles, and monitor progress remotely.
  • Mobil ID: A digital ID app that provides convenient access to campus buildings and resources using your phone.
  • Timely Care: A 24/7 telehealth service offering mental health support, counseling, and other wellness resources.
  • CourseForum: A platform where students can view and share class and professor ratings, GPA averages, and course feedback.
  • Register bike/scooter: A service through UPD (University Police Department) to register your bike or scooter for added security and recovery in case of theft.
  • Wahoo Welcome Schedule: (Netbadge Login Required) orientation.virginia.edu/2024-wahoo-welcome-schedule
